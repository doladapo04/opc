There's so much potential trapped in one pager websites that hasn't been unveiled yet. One pager websites present the following opportunities:

  • Saves money by cutting hosting expenses meant for multiple pager websites
  • Saves time meant for loading webpages. Pages are ready and loaded
  • Eye catching visuals for potential customers
  • Flexible and easy to use for site users
  • Consice and brief presentations for organizations

Demo Button

Bookflip.JS

Just picture it... A website that flips like a book rather than vertically. Pretty cool right? It's kind of like this site and it's just as cool. Go check it out.

view demo

FancyNav.JS

We're tired of seeing the same old hamburger navigation menus everyday. This is a library that allows you to have a wide range of nav menu options tailored to suit your site. Whatever your pick is, just choose and you won't be disappointed.

view demo

Suide.JS

"Suide" is an abbreviation meaning "Site Guide". I know, who came up with the name if these things right? Anyway, it's a cool thingy that allows you to show your site users how to use your site. This is very cool and useful especially for unusual sites like this one. Think of it as a welcome mat to the door of your home.

view demo

VScroll.JS

It's similar to "Bookflip.JS". The difference is it allows you to scroll for a long time horizontally rather than one page at a time.

view demo

DScroll.JS

This is quite similar to "VScroll.JS". The difference is it allows you to scroll diagonally rather than vertically.

view demo
